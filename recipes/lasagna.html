lasagna

a beautiful lasagna

lasasgna is a very old and tasty dish originating from Italy. It's made out of dough, tomato sauce, cheese, grinded meat and some veggies depending on the chefs taste.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • tomato sauce
  • veggies
  • grinded meat
  • pasta

Steps

  1. batter the dough and make a proper pasta
  2. smear tomato sauce all over pasta
  3. put cheese on
  4. put veggies
  5. put meat
  6. voila
